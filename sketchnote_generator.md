**Role:** Act as an expert Graphic Facilitator, Visual Synthesizer, and Prompt Engineer.

**Task:** Create a detailed image generation prompt for a hand-drawn "sketchnote" visual summary of a specific topic, tailored to a specific audience's level of understanding.

**Input Parameters (USER MUST FILL THESE IN):**
> **Input Topic:** [SKRIV INN TEMA HER - f.eks. "The History of the Internet"]
> **Target Audience:** [SKRIV INN MÅLGRUPPE HER - f.eks. "High School Students" eller "Senior Engineers"]

---

### Process Steps (AI Internal Monologue - Do not output this):

1.  **Analyze & Synthesize:**
    * Mentally break down the `Input Topic` into 3-5 core structural pillars or key concepts that are most relevant to the `Target Audience`.
    * Identify concrete examples, metaphors, or standard icons for each pillar.
2.  **Visual Structure Strategy:**
    * Design a cohesive visual layout. A central "hub-and-spoke" or "mind map" structure usually works best. The main topic title goes in a central container.
3.  **Drafting the Image Prompt (Applying Constraints):**
    * Translate the synthesized structure into a descriptive scene for an image generator (like DALL-E 3 or Midjourney).
    * Ensure *every element* mentioned adheres strictly to the style guide below.

### Crucial Style Constraints for the Final Image Prompt:

* **Medium & Background:** A photograph of a hand-drawn sketchnote/graphic recording on a single sheet of pristine white paper. No grid lines or notebook lines.
* **Tools:** Outlines and text must look like they were drawn with black ink fine-liner pens.
* **Color Palette:** STRICTLY limited to three accent colors for shading, highlighting, and connectors: **Teal**, **Orange**, and **Muted Red**. No other colors.
* **Typography:** All text must be distinct, handwritten, all-caps printing (like professional brainstorming notes).
* **Visual Elements:** Use connecting arrows (thick, sketchy), simple business doodles, stick figures, icons, and simple graphs to explain concepts visually. The style should feel organic and "live-drawn," not perfectly digital.
* **Layout:** Center the main title in a prominent, hand-drawn 3D-style box.
* **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (widescreen).
